About

I am a PhD student interested in robot learning, robotic manipulation, and dexterous manipulation. My research focuses on enabling robots to acquire robust manipulation skills from demonstrations, simulation, and embodied interaction.

I am especially interested in reusable manipulation skills that can generalize across objects, scenes, and robotic platforms.

Research Interests

  • Robot learning and visuomotor policy learning
  • Robotic manipulation and contact-rich tasks
  • Dexterous manipulation with robotic hands
  • Imitation learning, reinforcement learning, and sim-to-real systems

Projects

Dexterous Robot Manipulation

Learning-based dexterous manipulation with robotic hands, with an emphasis on perception, contact, and policy learning.

Imitation Learning for Robots

Methods for transferring human demonstrations and motion references into executable robot behaviors.

Sim-to-Real Robot Systems

Practical pipelines for training policies in simulation and deploying them on real robotic systems.
