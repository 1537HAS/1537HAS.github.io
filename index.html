About

Robotics researcher focused on learning-based manipulation.

I am a PhD student working at the intersection of robot learning, manipulation, and embodied intelligence. My research interests include dexterous manipulation, imitation learning, reinforcement learning, visuomotor policy learning, and sim-to-real robot systems.

I am particularly interested in how robots can learn reusable manipulation skills that generalize across objects, scenes, and hardware platforms.
